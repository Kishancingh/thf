Our Mission

To democratize entrepreneurial wisdom. We exist to ensure that geography and background never dictate a founder's ability to succeed. We bring high-level business strategies to the grassroots of India.

Our Vision

To become the largest repository of Indian startup stories from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities, creating a future where "Hidden Founders" are the new mainstream icons.

Who is THF For?

We speak the language of the builders.

Aspiring Founders

Individuals in Tier-2/3 cities looking for relatable role models and practical roadmaps.

Students & Dreamers

University students who want to break out of the traditional job mindset.

Early Stage Startups

Founders with an MVP who need guidance on scaling, funding, and hiring.
